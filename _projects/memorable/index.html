Memorable

Personalized entertainment for people with dementia

We are designing personalized entertainment for older adults in care homes facilitated by caretakers and family members. Memorable emphasizes personalized content for the residents as part of reminiscence therapy.

Video here when we have one

Features

The following are essential features of the application. Previous research has shown that simple puzzle games and familiar music are beneficial to people with dementia.

  • Reminiscence therapy puzzle activity
  • Reminiscence therapy music player
  • Personalized content for puzzles
  • Personalized content for music
  • Slideshow for casual image browsing
  • Feedback on residents' favourite content
