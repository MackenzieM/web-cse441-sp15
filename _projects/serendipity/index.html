What We Do

"At Serendipity, we want to provide the tools
to help everyday travelers explore the city of their choice on their own pace."

Explore

Our Exploratory Map View allows travelers to explore various attracitions and read recommended points of interests across multiple categories such as food, sights, hotels, museums, etc.

Navigate

When a point of interest is selected, the traveller can choose to navigate via walking, driving, or public transit, and the app will overlay routing and current GPS data on top of the map view to guide the traveller where they need to go, online or offline.

Never Get Lost

Once travelers set their Home Location, the app will provide the fastest and easiest guidence and navigation to bring them back Home whenever and wherever needed.
